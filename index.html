About Our Q&A Platform

VISION

  1. Teach Accurate Knowledge: To Teach the Accurate knowledge of God's word by the anointing of the Spirit so as to manifest the very Wisdom of Christ and communicate the same wisdom to the people.
  2. Reveal Christ: To reveal Christ through the thorough exposition of the Scriptures.
  3. Build Strong Believers: To Build a people armed with in-depth knowledge of the Scriptures and sound in the understanding of Doctrines.
  4. Exercise Authority: To raise believers who are aware of their rights and privileges in Christ and exercise the promises and authority of God's word.
  5. Deliverance Through Light: To bring about deliverance through the Light of God's word and Enlightenment by the Spirit of God.
  6. Raise Role Models: To raise unit members who are rooted in the word and can communicate it, role models both academically and spiritually with the help of the holy spirit.

How It Works

  • Ask Questions: Submit your questions anonymously or with your name
  • Get Answers: Church leadership and knowledgeable members provide thoughtful answers
  • Browse & Learn: Search through existing questions and answers
  • Grow Together: Learn from others' questions and insights

Guidelines

  • Be respectful and courteous in all questions and answers
  • Ask genuine questions seeking understanding
  • Keep questions focused on faith, doctrine, and Christian living
  • Be patient as we work to provide thoughtful responses
